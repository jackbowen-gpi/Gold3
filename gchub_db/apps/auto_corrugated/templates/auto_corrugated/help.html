Step by Step Instructions

  1. Form Entry: Enter all corrugated information into the Box Form. Click on the button 'Generate Box'.
    • Nine Digit Number: FSB part number
    • Fourteen Digit Number: SCC number
    • Box Dimensions: The dimensions of the box are outer dimensions pulled from the QAD system. They are populated automatically after selecting the case count.
    • Box Format: Left places the large panel on the far left. Right handed places the small panel on the far left.
    • SFI Content Stamp: If the box contents (cups) are SFI certified, check this to apply the SFI content stamp.
    • Create Slugs: Separates graphic elements into smaller slugs for platemaking purposes. Will not be generated in Preview PDF -- only in the approved PDF.
    • Platemaking: Select "None/Outsourced" if an outside vendor is going to make the plates. Otherwise, select the appropriate platemaker. If a platemaker is selected, a graphic artist in Clemson will produce the files needed to send for platemaking.
  2. Confirm Information: This will redirect you to a screen where you will see all the information you just entered, along with several choices down the right side:
    • Download Preview PDF - Downloads a PDF that is both watermarked and locked. This is for approval purposes only, and cannot be used for production.
    • Approve PDF - The preview PDF looks acceptable. Approving the preview creates a job in GOLD, and will bill the appropriate plant $100. Once approved, a production-quality PDF may be downloaded.
    • PDF Requires Changes by Artist - Most of the information on the PDF is correct, but some changes need to be made by a graphic artist. This will create a job in GOLD. Changes to be made should be entered into the upper right of the job page as a comment.
    • Edit Information - Edit the current box information. Unavailable once the box is approved.
    • Create Variant - Create a new corrugated box, starting with the form filled out with the information of the current box.
  3. Review PDF: Start by clicking on Download Preview PDF. This will allow you to open a locked and watermarked PDF for review. Inspect the information entered, and the elements placed onto the graphic.
  4. Make Selection: Once you have inspected the content, make your selection from one of the other options. (Approve, Requires Changes, Edit Information, or Create Variant.)
  5. Production: Once you have approved the PDF generated by the automated corrugated system, you may download a production-ready PDF that can be used to make plates. If you have checked that slugs should be made, a graphic artist in Clemson will use those slugs to prepare files that will be sent to platemaking in Shelbyville. Otherwise, the PDF can be sent to the plate vendor of your choosing.
